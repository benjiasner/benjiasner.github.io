Key Skills

Benji's highlighted skills.

Physics and Engineering

Through my major in Physics at the University of Chicago have hundreds of hours of experience designing physics experiments, working with complex digital and analog circuits, and building custom hardware. Additionally, I was certified by the University of Chicago to use their machine shop.

Software and Programming

I have extensive experience programming in Python, JavaScript, and Java. I have built complex algorithms utilizing complex mathetmatical conecpts and have worked on 3D modeling and visualization. I also have limited experience in computer architecture and design, as well as assembly.

Economics and Data Science

I am also majoring in Economics with a Data Science Specialization. I love studying securities trading, behavioral economics, price theory (Linear Programming), econometrics (panel and time series), and macroeconomics (Dynamic Programming).
