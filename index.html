EVIE

EVIE: EveryPlate's Very Intelligent Economizer

For my summer 2020 internship, I was fortunate enough to work at HelloFresh as a Data Science/Special Operations intern. My main project for the summer was a menu-planning algorithm for EveryPlate, a value-brand subsidiary of mealkit company HelloFresh. Using Python and linear programming methods (specifically the package PuLP), I created a 1500 line program that takes in customer/recipe data from google sheets and outputs an ideal recipe lineup for EveryPlate's subscribers. In all, it is estimated that my algorithm cut down the total time of menu-planning from upwards of 10 hours to 1 hour and saved HelloFresh and its customers money. It is currently in use at EveryPlate.

  • Date: June-August 2020
  • Client: HelloFresh
  • Category: Coding

Sunpouch Solar

My awesome attempt at solving global warming.

In order to make money for college, during the summer after I graduated high school I began designing what are called “Sunpouches”, a device that sticks on the back of an iPhone and charges it via solar power. On my own I designed the internal circuitry, sourced the raw materials (epoxy and molding equipment from Amazon, solar panels from Alibaba), did all branding/marketing, designed the packaging, and ended up selling a bunch of them to my friends.

  • Client: Myself
  • Category: Electrical Engineering and Prototyping
